Omelette



Description:

An omelette is a quick and easy way to take a healthy daily dose of eggs and spice it up a little
Pick up your eggs and lets get crackin'.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • 2 eggs
  • milk
  • salt
  • pepper
  • shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. crack eggs into mixing bowl
  2. add a splash of milk
  3. salt and pepper to taste
  4. add egg mixture to buttered and heated skillet
  5. cook omellete on both sides
  6. transfer from skillet to plate
  7. immediately top with shredded cheese
