Tacos



Description:

Street tacos can be a quick, fun, satsifying and tasty treat, whether you're visiting a restaurant, food truck, or whipping them up at home.
Today we will show you how to do the latter.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef skirt steaks
  • 10 corn tortillas
  • beef marinade
  • onions
  • cooking oil
  • limes (optional)
  • pico de gallo (optional)

Instructions

  1. marinate skirt steaks in the morning until time to cook
  2. chop onions
  3. slice limes
  4. grill skirt steaks to preference
  5. grill onions in tinfoil
  6. oil pan and warm tortillas on both sides
  7. chop your skirt steaks
  8. fill tacos with pico, steak and onion
  9. top with fresh squeezed lime
