Chicken Salad



Description:

Chicken Salad acts as a fountain of youth for that dry and bland rotisserie chicken in the fridge.
But you won't have to travel to the ends of the Earth to follow along with this simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • Rotisserie Chicken
  • Mayonnaise
  • salt
  • pepper
  • onions
  • celery

Instructions

  1. chop chicken into bite sized cubes
  2. chop celery and onions to bite sized
  3. add chicken and vegetables to mixing bowl
  4. add Mayonnaise to preferred consistency
  5. salt and pepper to taste
